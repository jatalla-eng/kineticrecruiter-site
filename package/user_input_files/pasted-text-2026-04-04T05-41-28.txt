HERO ILLUSTRATION — SPECIFIC VISUAL DIRECTION
This is the most important visual on the page. It must convey the concept of candidates flowing through a recruitment pipeline powered by AI. Match the style and feel of the reference mockup provided.
Composition:
The illustration shows a diagonal or gently curving flow from the bottom-left to the top-right of the graphic area. Three visible pipeline stages are labelled along this flow: "Sourcing" (bottom-left), "Interviewing" (middle), and "Offer" (top-right). The flow moves upward and to the right, suggesting forward momentum and progression.
Candidate cards:
At each stage, place 1-2 candidate card elements. Each card is a small white rounded rectangle with a subtle drop shadow, containing:

A circular avatar placeholder (use simple coloured circles with abstract person silhouettes — teal, amber, or muted violet fills)
2-3 short horizontal lines below the avatar representing name/details (like a wireframe placeholder)
Some cards should have a small teal badge in the corner suggesting a match score or AI indicator

The cards should feel like they're "in motion" — slightly rotated (2-3 degrees), with some overlap, as if they're being carried along the flow.
Flow arrows / path:
Connect the stages with smooth, flowing curved arrows or swooping lines in teal and light cyan. These are not rigid straight arrows — they should be organic, sweeping curves that feel like a river or a breeze carrying the cards forward. Use varying opacity (some arrows more solid, some faded) and varying thickness to create depth. Some arrows can have a slight gradient from teal to cyan.
The arrows are the primary visual device conveying "kinetic" energy and momentum. They should feel dynamic, not static.
Stage labels:
"Sourcing", "Interviewing", and "Offer" appear as small text labels near their respective card clusters. Use dark navy text, small/medium weight, not inside boxes — just floating labels positioned near the relevant point in the flow.
Decorative elements:

Small teal dots or circles scattered along the flow path (like particles or data points in motion)
A subtle document/resume icon near the Sourcing stage (a small page icon with lines on it)
A subtle sparkle or AI star icon near one of the candidate cards to suggest AI processing
Light cyan or teal abstract shapes in the background of the illustration area (soft blurred circles or gradient blobs) to add depth without clutter

Colour palette for the illustration:

Teal (#0d8488) for primary arrows and accents
Flow Cyan (light aqua) for secondary arrows and background shapes
White for candidate cards
Amber for one or two avatar circles (adds warmth and variety)
Muted violet for one avatar circle
Dark navy for label text
Very light teal/cyan for background gradient blobs

Style:
Clean, modern, flat with subtle depth (soft shadows on cards, gradient on arrows). NOT 3D. NOT isometric. NOT hand-drawn or sketchy. Think product illustration style — the kind you'd see on Linear, Notion, or Vercel's marketing pages. Airy, spacious, confident.
What to avoid:

No stock photos of people
No laptop/phone device mockups in the hero
No screenshots of the actual product UI
No rigid flowchart or box-and-arrow diagrams
No heavy gradients or dark backgrounds in the hero area
No clip art or overly cartoonish illustration style

Below hero: Subtle social proof bar — "TRUSTED BY TEAMS THAT MOVE FAST" in small caps grey text, centred. Below it, 4-5 generic greyscale placeholder logo shapes (simple rounded rectangles or abstract marks — NOT real brand logos like Microsoft or Spotify). This section is a placeholder that will be updated with real client logos later.

This is a marketing/landing page, not the actual ATS application. It should feel like a product website (think stripe.com, linear.app, notion.so) — not a web app.
Logo: Use the attached PNG file exactly as provided. Do not attempt to recreate, redraw, or restyle the logo. Place it as an image wherever the logo appears. For the favicon, crop to just the teal "K" icon portion.
The hero illustration is the most critical visual on the page. Follow the detailed specification above precisely — flowing candidate cards through Sourcing → Interviewing → Offer stages with sweeping teal/cyan arrows conveying momentum. Reference the attached brand mockup for the intended style and energy.
The website mockup concept in the brand assets shows "Put momentum behind your hiring" as a headline — use "The ATS that actually understands your candidates" instead as the primary headline. The momentum language can be used elsewhere on the site.
Do not include fake client logos (Microsoft, Spotify, etc.) as shown in the mockup concept placeholder. Use generic grey placeholder shapes or remove the social proof bar until real logos are available.
All copy is written in Australian/British English (organisation, not organization; colour, not color; analyse, not analyze).
Keep the page fast. Minimise heavy animations. Subtle fade-in on scroll is fine. No parallax. No auto-playing video.
The comparison tables are critical — they need to be scannable and make KineticRecruiter the obvious winner visually (teal column highlight, competitor columns muted).
