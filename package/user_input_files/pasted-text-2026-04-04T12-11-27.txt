### FAQ Section

**Can I change plans at any time?**
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period.

**What happens when I hit my candidate limit?**
You'll see a notification as you approach your limit. Once you reach it, you won't be able to add new candidates until you upgrade. Your existing data is never affected.

**What happens when the trial ends?**
If you don't cancel during the 7-day trial, your subscription begins automatically on the plan you selected. Cancel anytime during the trial at no charge.

**Is there a setup or implementation fee?**
No. There are no setup fees, implementation fees, or training costs. You sign up and start using the platform immediately. Agency plan includes a dedicated onboarding session.

**Do I need to pay extra for AI features?**
No. Every AI feature is included in every plan. There are no AI add-ons or premium tiers.

**How does seat-based billing work?**
You pay per active user. Add or remove seats at any time from the billing dashboard. Deactivated users don't count toward your seat total.

**Do you offer annual billing?**
Yes. Annual billing saves approximately 17% compared to monthly. All plans are available on both cycles.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
All major credit cards via Stripe. Invoicing is available for Agency plan customers with 10 or more seats.

**Is there a minimum contract?**
No. Monthly plans have no minimum commitment. Annual plans are billed upfront for 12 months.

## VISUAL RULES — APPLY TO EVERY PAGE

1. **No stock photography of people anywhere on the site.** Use illustrations, UI mockups, or icons only.
2. **Hero images are right-aligned** in two-column layouts (text left, image right). On mobile, image stacks below text.
3. **Hero images are illustrations or stylised UI mockups**, never real screenshots.
4. **Candidate cards in every illustration must include a teal match score badge.** This is the product's visual signature.
5. **Consistent card style everywhere:** white background, 8px border radius, shadow (0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)).
6. **Teal (#0d8488) is the only primary accent.** Amber and violet appear only in avatar circles for variety.
7. **Every hero image must pass the 5-second test:** someone looking at only the image for 5 seconds should understand what the page is about.
8. **No decorative elements that don't represent something in the product.** No abstract blobs, hexagons, or circuit patterns.
9. **Footer on every page:** remove "Created by MiniMax Agent". Keep logo, nav links, and copyright.
10. **Australian/British English everywhere:** organisation (not organization), colour (not color), analyse (not analyze).
