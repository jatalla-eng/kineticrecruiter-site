## 1. Positioning and Voice

### One-liner
AI-powered ATS that turns a 6-hour CV pile into a ranked shortlist in 60 seconds — built for recruitment agencies.

### Core message hierarchy

| Priority | Message | Use when |
|---|---|---|
| P1 | Screening 200 CVs took 6 hours. Now it takes 60 seconds. | Pain + transformation hooks |
| P2 | Natural-language search across your entire candidate database. | Feature demo, differentiator |
| P3 | Built for agencies. Unlimited clients, Chrome extension, client CRM. | Agency-specific targeting |
| P4 | Try it free for 7 days. No credit card. | CTAs, retargeting |

### Voice (from product site)

- Direct. Short sentences. No hedging.
- No marketing jargon ("leverage", "synergy", "end-to-end", "game-changer", "streamline").
- Specific numbers beat vague claims (say "60 seconds" not "fast").
- Self-deprecating humour about recruiter pain is welcome. Corporate-speak is not.

### Words to use
AI, resume, CV, candidate, shortlist, score, match, screen, pipeline, agency, recruiter, pile, rank, parse, client, placement, fees, billable.

### Words to avoid
Solution, platform (as filler), streamline, robust, cutting-edge, revolutionary, game-changer, leverage, utilise, end-to-end, enterprise-grade, best-in-class, holistic, synergy, talent acquisition professional.

## 2. Content Pillars and Hook Formulas

Five pillars, mapped to R.I.C.K. archetypes from `ugc-framework/prompts/script-generator.md`.

### Pillar 1 — Pain (TOF / cold)

**Use:** cold prospecting on Meta + TikTok
**Hook archetypes:** The mistake, Broad polariser, Myth bust

Example hooks:
- "If you've ever stared at a pile of 200 CVs at 11pm, this is for you."
- "The mistake 90% of recruiters make when screening CVs."
- "Unpopular opinion: keyword-search on your ATS is actively losing you placements."
- "Stop manually scoring candidates. You're doing 6 hours of work a tool does in 60 seconds."

### Pillar 2 — Transformation (TOF/MOF)

**Use:** broad audiences, first-time viewers
**Hook archetypes:** Proof-first, Experience hook

Example hooks:
- "I went from 6 hours of CV screening to 60 seconds. Here's what changed."
- "I tried KineticRecruiter for 30 days. Here's what actually happened."
- "I placed 3 candidates in 5 days using one feature."

### Pillar 3 — Feature demo (MOF)

**Use:** video viewers 25%+, retargeting warm audiences
**Hook archetypes:** Nobody talks about, Simple method

Example hooks:
- "Nobody in recruitment talks about natural-language candidate search, but it changed how I source."
- "The simplest way to rank 500 candidates for one role."
- "Watch this AI read 200 CVs in real-time and tell me which 5 to call."

### Pillar 4 — Social proof (MOF/BOF)

**Use:** retargeting, pricing-page visitors
**Hook archetypes:** Experience hook, Proof-first

Example hooks:
- "Small agency, 3 recruiters, 40% more placements this quarter. Here's what they changed."
- "This is how [agency name] hired 3× faster."
- "400 candidates, 2 minutes, 1 shortlist. This is what modern recruiting looks like."

### Pillar 5 — Offer (BOF)

**Use:** retargeting, bottom-funnel search intent
**Hook archetypes:** Simple method, What I'd do over

Example hooks:
- "If I started my agency again, I'd skip every ATS except this one."
- "7-day free trial. No credit card. Cancel in one click."
- "The fastest way to know if AI actually helps you hire: try it for a week."

### Pillar 6 — Pricing Clarity (TOF/MOF) — uncontested angle

**Use:** cold prospecting + retargeting on Meta where per-seat price shock drives scroll-stop. Competitor intel confirms every agency-ATS competitor except Manatal charges per-seat; none are running anti-per-seat creative. This is the clearest unclaimed messaging lane in the competitive set.
**Hook archetypes:** Unpopular opinion, Myth bust, The mistake

**Pricing anchor for Pillar 6:** Professional plan ($59/mo monthly OR $49/mo on annual) is the comparison anchor — it's the right fit for a 5-recruiter agency (unlimited jobs, unlimited clients, 1,000 candidates). Lead with annual price for max impact.

Example hooks:
- "Your ATS charges $150 per recruiter. Ours is $49 flat for the whole agency on annual."
- "Unpopular opinion: per-seat pricing is an ATS tax. From $24/mo on annual covers your whole team."
- "Stop paying per recruiter. Pay for software, not seats. From $24/mo on annual."
- "Every ATS bill I had went up when we hired. Ours stays flat. From $29/mo, $24/mo on annual."
- "Quick math: 5 recruiters × $150/mo = $9,000/yr. KR Professional on annual = $588/yr. Same software."

## 6. Specs Reference

### Meta Reels / IG Feed video
- Dimensions: 1080 × 1920 (9:16)
- Duration: 15s (standard), 30s (max performant for mid-funnel)
- File: MP4, H.264
- Max: 4GB
- Safe zone: 250px top + 400px bottom (avoid critical text in UI overlay zones)
- Captions: burned in, 80pt+, center-vertical safe zone
- Audio: include even though most will watch muted

### TikTok video
- Dimensions: 1080 × 1920 (9:16)
- Duration: 15-30s optimal for Spark Ads
- File: MP4, H.264
- Safe zone: top 150px + bottom 400px (account for TikTok UI overlay)
- Captions: burned in, use TikTok-native-feeling font (system sans)
- No watermarks from other platforms (TikTok penalizes visibly reposted content)

### Meta Feed static
- Square: 1080 × 1080
- File: JPG or PNG, ≤4MB
- Text-in-image: <20% is no longer a hard rule but readability drops above 30%
- Use high contrast — auto-preview on Feed is small

### Google Responsive Display (if added later, Month 2+)
- Landscape: 1200 × 628
- Square: 1200 × 1200
- Logo: 1200 × 1200 or 1200 × 300
- Headlines (30 chars) × 5, Long headlines (90 chars) × 1, Descriptions (90 chars) × 5

## 8. Creative Iteration and Fatigue

### Fatigue signals
- Frequency >3.0 on Meta prospecting
- CTR dropped 25% from peak (measure over 7-day rolling window)
- CPM rising 30%+ with CVR flat or falling
- Comments shifting negative or ad disappearing from Feed

### Refresh cadence
- Weeks 1-2: ship 5 videos + 2 statics at launch
- Weeks 3-4: review performance, double down on top 2 pillars (likely Pain + Feature demo)
- Weeks 5-6: ship 3 more videos in winning pillars, 2 new statics
- Week 7+: weekly rolling creative refresh — 1 new video per week minimum

### Variant strategy per winning creative

Once a video hits top-performer status, produce 3 variants before retiring it:
1. **Hook swap:** same body, different R.I.C.K. hook archetype
2. **User archetype swap:** same script, different actor/demographic
3. **CTA swap:** same setup, test "Try free for 7 days" vs "Start free trial" vs "See pricing"

This triples the mileage from one creative concept and isolates what's working.

## 10. Legal and Compliance

- No testimonial quotes unless sourced and documented. Paraphrased common pain points are fine.
- No implied endorsement from LinkedIn, Bullhorn, or other competitors.
- Trial terms must match what the landing page says (7 days, no card).
- GDPR: UK/EU targeting requires CMP and cookie consent on landing pages — not launching there Month 1, so defer until Month 4 expansion.
- Australian spam law: all CTAs lead to landing pages with privacy policy and contact details (already on site).
